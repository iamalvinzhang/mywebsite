Gaming has always been one of my favorite pastimes. What started with Super Mario Bros turned into a lifelong passion for gaming - particularly strategy games. Gaming was a way for me to relax with friends but also a place where I could channel my competitive energy.

This was true especially when it came to League of Legends. Within 2 years of playing the game seriously, I found myself rank 90 out of 28 million players. I started a boosting (playing on another person's account to increase their rank) business soon after.

This was where I first came across marketing in a real life setting. Originally, I was going to design a boosting website but there were already too many eloboosting websites. Investing in SEO for good website placement would not have been worth it. Instead, I advertised on social media platforms - Discord, WeChat and Twitter.

I've also gotten the opportunity to be a consultant to a few professional Academy and LCS League of Legends players as well as a few twitch streamers. Am also a stereotypical twitch moderator for a few channels!
